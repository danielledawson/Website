Grandmothers Creations

My nan learned how to make these traditional medicines from our aunty Wata and my great gran Ethel and gramps Jimmy. She learned to take only what she needs. She goes out in the forests picks out stinging nettle, devils club, horsetail, twin berry alder, plantain, and spruce pitch. She then drys them out or puts them in olive oil for ten days, then simmers on the stove in a bath water for two days.

Heres a list of some of the things my gran has created!

  • Stinging nettle and horsetail shampoo
  • Stinging nettle and horsetail tea
  • Plantain salve
  • Devils club salve
  • Twin berry alder and plantain salve and oils
  • Spruce pitch salve
  • Lip bombs
  • Bath bombs
  • Loom beaded native designs on hats
  • Loom beaded lanyards and keychains
